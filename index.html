In the summer of 2012, I joined Gainesville's hackerspace "Skillhouse". I was amazed by the projects I saw there. Drawing bots, 3D printers, hacked LED christmas lights, home server security systems, etc. And it was all possible because of programming. I was fortunate that the people who created these amazing contraptions were also more than willing to teach and tutor anyone excited to learn. I surrounded myself with this culture of creating and teaching and soon became on the Board of Directors for the hackerspace. Fast forward to now and I'm a full time Ruby on Rails developer at a startup that is quickly leaving it's infancy. Web development did not come easily to me but I was hooked on honing my skills. My passion now is to help those who want to learn.
